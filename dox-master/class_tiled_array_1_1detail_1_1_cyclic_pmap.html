Maps cyclically a sequence of indices onto a 2-d matrix of processes.

Consider a sequence of indices , organized into a matrix, , in row-major form, i.e. index corresponds to . Similarly, a sequence of processes is organized into a matrix with rows and columns, i.e process . Then index maps to process

Note
This class is used to map tile indices to processes.
